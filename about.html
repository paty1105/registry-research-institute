About

Evidence Before Opinion.

The Registry Research Institute is an independent educational research project dedicated to collecting, organizing, and analyzing public evidence about modern sex offender registration laws.

RRI is built around a simple premise: laws that affect public safety, constitutional rights, families, housing, employment, and reintegration should be evaluated through rigorous evidence rather than assumption, fear, or political shorthand.

Editorial Standard: RRI separates research summaries, legal analysis, commentary, and personal narrative. Every factual claim should be traceable to a source.

Mission

To advance evidence-based understanding of registration systems through empirical research, legal analysis, state-by-state documentation, and public education.

Core Values

  • Accuracy over rhetoric
  • Transparency in sourcing
  • Fair representation of competing evidence
  • Respect for public safety and constitutional limits
  • Human dignity for victims, families, and registrants
