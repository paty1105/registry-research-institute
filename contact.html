Submit research or collaborate.

PRI is built from primary sources, and a great deal of the most useful material comes from people working directly with it. If you have research, a court record, a state audit, a dataset, or a correction, send it.

This is not a crisis line and not a legal service. If you need immediate help, contact local emergency services. If you are seeking legal representation, PRI cannot provide it or refer you to counsel.

This routes your message. It is not published anywhere.
If you are submitting research, a citation or link is more useful than a summary.

Submissions go to the Institute only. PRI does not publish the identity of anyone who submits material without their explicit written agreement.

Corrections

PRI publishes corrections openly, as set out in the Research Charter. If you have found an error, a misread source, or a claim that outruns its citation, choose "Submitting a correction" above and quote the passage. Corrections are the fastest way to improve the site and are treated as such.
