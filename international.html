Comparative Prevention

Comparative Prevention

Other democracies face the same problem and have made different choices. This section will compare how they invest in prevention, structure treatment access, manage risk and supervision, handle public notification and individualized review, and what outcomes they report.

The comparison is useful precisely because these are not hypothetical alternatives. They are operating systems in countries with comparable legal traditions, and several of them publish outcome data that the United States does not.
