Evidence That Prevents Harm

Independent research for safer communities and effective prevention.

The Prevention Research Institute studies what stops sexual harm before it happens, and whether the policies built to address it can show that they work. That means education and safeguarding, help available before anyone is hurt, treatment that produces documented change, and public spending held to a measurable standard.

Fewer victims is the only outcome that counts. We organize the evidence so that decisions can be made on it rather than on assumption.
