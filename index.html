Evidence That Prevents Harm

Independent research for safer communities and effective prevention.

The Prevention Research Institute studies what actually prevents sexual harm, reduces reoffending, supports successful reintegration, and protects families. We organize behavioral science, treatment research, legal analysis, policy data, and practical prevention strategies so public-safety decisions can be guided by measurable outcomes rather than fear or assumption.
