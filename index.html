Evidence Before Opinion

Independent research on modern sex offender registration laws.

The Registry Research Institute examines whether modern registry laws reflect the best available legal, scientific, and empirical evidence. We organize research, court decisions, state laws, policy data, and visual evidence so the public can evaluate registry policy through facts rather than assumptions.

Does the evidence support the way modern registry laws work today?

RRI does not begin with conclusions. It begins with questions: how registration systems changed, what the science shows about risk and desistance, how administrative violations are counted, and whether policy outcomes match public-safety claims.
